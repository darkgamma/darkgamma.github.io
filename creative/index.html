Creative

an Index

This is, in short, an index to all my creative content, and there's lots! We're still moving in, so expect a lot of missing content. The Creative section is divided into three large topical chunks:

  • Languages — containing the conlangs I've made
  • Conworlding — containing the worlds I've designed
  • Writing — the informal poetry and prose I've decided to share on here

Due to the rather interconnected nature of all the material that goes into here, all the pages are (or, at least, should be) heavily inter-linked, enabling easy navigation between topically close content. Practically speaking, all the conlangs I've thus far made exist in some sort of conworld, generally built around the languages, and as such practically nothing is left contextless in that way. Much of my writing, as well, is very much closely linked to the conworlds and conlangs I've made, and many of the stories have hyperlinks to relevant parts of other pages.

In the top right corner of the navbar you can find the general area (of the three large subdivisions above) that you're in, as well as the general page (or, in some cases, category of pages) you're on right now.
